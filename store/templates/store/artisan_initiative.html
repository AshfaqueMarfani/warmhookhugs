The Artisan Initiative

Empowering skilled women artisans across Pakistan through dignified work, fair wages, and the preservation of traditional craft techniques.

Our Mission

Warm Hook Hugs is more than a brand — it's a vehicle for change. We believe that the extraordinary crochet and knitting skills found across Pakistan deserve recognition, fair compensation, and a global stage.

Through our Hub-and-Spoke model, we partner with women artisans in peri-urban and rural areas across Sindh and Punjab. We supply premium yarns, standardized patterns, and quality training — they bring their incredible skill, creativity, and dedication.

The result? Heirloom-quality products that carry the warmth and soul of the hands that made them, while providing sustainable livelihoods for artisan families.
