Return & Exchange Policy

Eligible for Return

  • Items in unused, original condition with tags attached.
  • Return request made within 7 days of delivery.
  • Items received damaged, defective, or incorrect.

Not Eligible for Return

  • Custom/personalized orders.
  • Items that have been used, washed, or altered.
  • Items returned after the 7-day window.

How to Return

  1. Contact us via WhatsApp or email within 7 days of delivery.
  2. Provide your order ID and reason for return.
  3. Ship the item back to our Karachi address (customer bears return shipping unless item is defective).
  4. Refund is processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item.
